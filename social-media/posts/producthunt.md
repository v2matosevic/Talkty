## Description

Talkty turns your voice into text without sending your microphone anywhere. Press a global hotkey, speak, and the words land on your clipboard, ready to paste in any app. Whisper runs locally with GPU acceleration, and the audio is discarded the moment it becomes text. No account, no cloud, no telemetry.

It is also built for people who code by talking to an AI agent: an optional Prompting mode turns a rough dictation into a clean, structured prompt for tools like Claude Code, Cursor, and Codex. Free and open source, on both Windows and macOS.

## First comment (maker)

Hi Product Hunt. I made Talkty because I wanted to dictate without my voice leaving my machine. Everything runs on device by default, the optional cloud and AI-prompt features are off until you turn them on, and both the Windows and macOS apps are MIT licensed.

It started as a tool for talking to coding agents, but it works anywhere you can type. I would love your feedback, and the repos are open if you want to look under the hood or contribute.
